DIWALI

Diwali is one of the most popular and joyful festivals celebrated in India.

It is known as the festival of lights and symbolizes the victory of light over darkness and good over evil. On this day, people clean their homes, decorate them with diyas and lights, and wear new clothes.

People celebrate diwali by bursting crackers and sharing sweets with friends and family.

Diwali teaches us the importance of happiness, unity, and spreading positivity in life.
